These are flashcards for students in my Philosophy 112.

The flashcards are plaintext files. Each line contains two items, separated by a colon, e.g., 

~~P ∴ P; Double Negation Elimination (DNE)

The item before the colon is meant to be the "front" of the flashcard. The item after the colon is meant to be the "back" of the flashcard.

Various flashcard apps are able to import such files, including Anki, which is free and cross-platform. There are also web-based services that can import such files, like Cram.
